About SimLearner

We fill the gap between expensive coaching packages with overflows of information and a driver who wants to become faster without needing to analyze hundreds of parameters on his own. We focus on giving you **live feedback and coaching for the lowest price possible** while you can just drive and get the laps in.

Our Coaching Focus

Track Knowledge & Lines

Mastering the ideal racing lines for every corner to maximize speed and efficiency.

Braking Techniques

Learning proper trailbraking to achieve later and more effective braking points.

Throttle Application

Developing smooth and controlled throttle application for optimal car stability and exit speed.

ABS/TC Settings

Understanding and adjusting ABS/Traction Control settings for different corners and conditions.

Setup Tweaks

Making small, impactful setup changes to fine-tune the car to your driving style.

Competitive Racing Prep

Getting you ready for competitive racing events and free public lobbies.

Overtaking & Race Craft

Learning how to closely follow, cleanly overtake, and judge moves outside the racing line.

Visual & Audio Feedback

Utilizing visual, FFB, and audio cues from the simulation to improve your lap.

Error Correction

Identifying and correcting common driving mistakes in real-time.
